Despite studying art as young, I went back to art in 2018 when I took a confessional embroidery course. There I exercised the materialization of thoughts and feelings on the screen, with thread and needle. Embroidery stitches are free, with some more familiar like the back stitch and others.

Here I also present digital art experiments that I have been doing to understand this new movement. For me art is therapy and an important form of communication.
